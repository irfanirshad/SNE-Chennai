About SN Enterprises

We are one of the upcoming commercial property consultants located in Chennai. We specialize in rentals of office spaces & warehouses in and around Chennai, we currently manage an active database of more than 10,000 properties in office space, 500 properties in warehouse / factory / commercial land, guest houses & business centre's. We have been in this business for the past 11 Years and are well known for our highest standards of Service, Trustworthiness, Transparency and Professionalism.

We at S N Enterprises strongly believe that "Once a Client is Always a Client" and have a strong vision and mission to achieve customer satisfaction. We do periodically update our property database and employees so as to meet the current needs of our customers. We build a lifetime relationship with our customers and treat everyone with care, respect & fairness.
